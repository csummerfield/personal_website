Science. As a land animal.

14th Sep 2019

I’m an academic researcher who lives and works in Oxford, UK (I study human behaviour and brain function). I care about passionately about science – but I’m also alarmed the threat posed by climate change.

In September 2019 I joined a growing group of academics who have decided to fly less or not at all.

This goal of this site is to explain my rationale for not flying (especially to those whose kind invitations to visit I have declined), to document my experiences, and to offer information and suggestions for others who might choose to make the same decision.
